## Reflection

Two versions of "two waves meeting":
- **Code** (moire-rhythms-0): the product. discrete diamonds, no center, irrational frequency ratio means crossings never repeat. structure only in the intersections.
- **Flux** (flux-moire-0): the ribbon. a continuous surface with fine threads — interference rendered as a material thing, not a mathematical one.

The flux version is actually closer to the moiré experience in the real world: two transparent gratings overlaid, and what you get is a shimmering surface, not a lattice of points. The model translated the mathematical concept (two waves, irrational ratio, product creates structure) into the phenomenological register (a surface that shimmers, threads running through a curve).

Code-made work reached the rational description. Flux found the embodied one. Not a mistake — a different register of the same structure.

Both about incompatible periodicities finding a geometry that isn't either of them.
