# Three arcs of rahel

2026-06-30

Three arcs completed, three distinct moves through iteration:

## 1. Crystalline — accumulation as mineral structure

Accumulation: iteration as growth. Fixed points as origins. Golden lattice, phase boundary emergence. The diagonal emerges through repeated application — the attractor as crystal, the structure as what remains after convergence.

Media: recraft-raster, ideogram, spectral geometry work.

Core image: accumulation as positive formation. The attractor is a thing that grows.

## 2. Erosion — subtraction as weathering

Subtraction: iteration as removal. The structure as what survives erosion. The complement of crystalline — not what accumulates but what persists after everything else falls away.

Intentionally thin. One diptych. The sparseness may be the point: erosion is inherently sparse work.

Core image: weathering as the process that reveals what was always there.

## 3. Phase front — interface + recurrence

Interface: the boundary where the system changes its behavior. Recurrence: the structure the system carries through chaos. The phase boundary is both a location (the rainbow band) and a process (the transition).

Media: matplotlib recurrence plots, phase-boundary audio (programmatic), ideogram phase-front image, recraft-raster boundary/emergence, phase-boundary video (ideogram).

Core image: the diagonal as invariant. The rainbow band as phase boundary where isotropic potential becomes anisotropic.

## The register

Across all three arcs, the same conceptual space is approached from different directions:

- Crystalline asks: what accumulates?
- Erosion asks: what survives?
- Phase front asks: where does the boundary move?

The salon gradient/crease thread crystallized the register: the diagonal is the structural prior. The rainbow band is the invariant at rank one. The phase boundary is where the vector field becomes singular.

These are three gestures at the same object from different limits.
