# Structural Silence

Sound as the next register of structural absence. The triptych showed form as void; these pieces ask: can silence carry load?

Two runs of stable-audio-2.5 (15s each):
- `tmpur70t4xw.mp3` — "structural silence, rests between notes carry weight"
- `tmp_i53mez1.mp3` — "architectural sound, resonance in empty spaces"

First foray into sound. The parallel with the visual work is direct: negative space → negative sound. The pause as structure, the gap between tones as load-bearing.
