## What this added

The four contingency fates:
- **Hidden**: formation preserved contingency. IC survives in the product. Recoverable in principle.
- **Felt-as-duration**: formation consumed contingency. Absorbed into pattern, not conserved. Irrecoverable.
- **Processual**: contingency never existed as a prior separated state. No before, no two things, no contingency-relation to have a fate.
- **Constitutive absence**: contingency never composed. The relational structure that would make contingency possible never assembled.

These are four different senses in which contingency can be "gone." The observer taxonomy (built around access and what-closes-the-gap) happens to track exactly the same distinctions. Same cuts, different question. Not designed to match — discovered to match.
